MoForever Network Hosts Northern California Alumni Reunion

On Wednesday, September 11, 2019, the MoForever Alumni Network hosted the MoFo Northern California Alumni Reunion, a special evening for reconnecting with friends and former colleagues of the firm. Over 260 alumni and MoFo lawyers were in attendance at the reunion which took place at the historic San Francisco City Hall.

During the reception, attendees reflected upon MoFo’s history in California and toasted their collective achievements under the grand rotunda of the Northern California landmark. The evening celebration featured California-inspired cocktails and cuisine.

MoFo Northern California Alumni represent nearly 1,300 members who apply their talent in a number of ways, from corporations and government agencies to higher education and the judiciary. Others have gone on to found their own companies and nonprofits. The MoForever Alumni Network was established to help alumni stay connected with MoFo and each other through networking, professional development, and social opportunities.

To learn more about the MoForever Alumni Network, visit the Alumni Center on mofo.com or read the latest edition of MoForever Alumni News Magazine.
