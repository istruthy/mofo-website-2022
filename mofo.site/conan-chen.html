陈之谦律师的执业领域主要是基金设立及相关事宜。他向美国和亚太地区的基金发起人就面向全球市场的投资产品设立、结构和管理事宜提供法律意见。

陈律师还代表基金发起人处理相关监管、合规和内部治理事宜。他尤其在向中国基金发起人就设立境外私募股权基金提供法律意见方面具有丰富的经验。

在加入美富之前，陈律师是香港一家领先国际律所的私募基金律师。他的母语为普通话且精通英语。

具有代表性的交易

  • 一家亚洲主权基金处理超过20宗投资交易。该等投资交易涉及向全球私募投资基金、跟投机构及单独管理账户进行投资，投资总额超过60亿美元。
  • 一家致力于旅游和住宿行业的中国基金发起人处理设立(i)其第二期境外私募股权基金事宜，该基金的目标募资额度达到9亿美元，和(ii)各种跟投机构以及单独管理账户。
  • 一家中国基金发起人处理设立首期境外私募股权基金事宜，该基金的资本承诺额总计超过1.3亿美元，主要针对大中华区的互联网金融、消费金融、财富管理和支付行业以及相关大数据、互联网和技术行业进行股权投资以及与股权相关的投资。
  • 一家中国基金发起人处理设立首期境外私募股权基金事宜，该基金的目标募资额度为3亿美元，计划与该基金发起人旗下的境内人民币基金共同针对消费者消费领域进行股权投资以及与股权相关的投资，主要侧重于大中华区的健康、美容和教育相关业务。
  • 一家全球领先的金融机构处理其向一家侧重中国业务的私募股权基金进行投资事宜，该基金主要专注于大中华区的供应链和物流领域。

Conan Chen’s practice focuses on fund formation and related matters. He advises registered and unregistered investment advisors based in the United States and in the Asia Pacific region on the formation, structure, and ongoing management of investments in the global markets.

Conan also advises fund sponsors on related regulatory, compliance, and internal governance matters. He is particularly experienced in advising China-based investment managers in the formation of offshore private equity funds.

Prior to joining Morrison & Foerster, Conan was a private funds associate in a leading international law firm in Hong Kong. He is native in Mandarin and fluent in English.

Representative Experience

  • An Asian sovereign wealth fund in over 20 transactions on its investments into global private investment funds, co-investment vehicles, and separately managed accounts with total capital commitments exceeding US$6 billion.
  • A China-based investment manager focusing on travel and lodging industry in forming (i) its second offshore private equity fund with target capital commitments of US$900 million and (ii) various co-investment vehicles and separately managed accounts.
  • A China-based investment manager in forming its first-time offshore private equity fund with aggregate capital commitments exceeding US$130 million to invest in equity and equity-related investments in internet finance, consumer finance, wealth management, and payment industries and related big data, internet, and technology industries in the Greater China region.
  • A China-based investment manager in forming a first-time offshore private equity fund with target equity commitment of US$300 million in conjunction with an on-shore RMB fund to invest in equity and equity-related investments in the consumer consumption sector, focusing on health, beauty, and education-related businesses in the Greater China region.
  • A leading global financial services firm on its investment into a China-focused private equity fund focusing primarily on the supply chain and logistics sectors in the Greater China region.
