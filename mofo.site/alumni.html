Alumni Events Gallery

  • 2019 MoFo Northern California Alumni Reunion Video
  • 2019 New York MoForever Women’s Network Alumnae Reception

    On Wednesday, February 13, MoFo’s New York women attorneys and alumnae gathered at New York’s City Winery to connect and network with former colleagues. The reception was hosted by MoFo’s Women’s Strategy Committee and the MoForever Women’s Network. Guests enjoyed a wine tasting and a sommelier-guided tour of the wine cellar.

  • 2018 Los Angeles Alumni Reception

    On Thursday, June 28, MoFo’s Los Angeles alumni met at Faith & Flower in the WaterMarke Tower to enjoy a great evening networking with former colleagues and friends.

  • 2018 San Diego Alumni Reception

    On Tuesday, June 19, MoFo’s San Diego alumni met at the Viewpoint Brewing Company in Del Mar to catch up with former colleagues and friends, and enjoy an evening of brews and games.

  • 2018 Northern California MoForever Women’s Network Alumnae Reception

    On May 2, more than 55 MoFo alumnae and current attorneys attended the Northern California Women’s Alumnae reception held at the Nobu Hotel Epiphany in Palo Alto. The reception was hosted by MoFo’s Women’s Strategy Committee and the MoForever Women’s Network.

  • 2017 MoFo Summit for Women In-House Counsel

    On April 20, more than 30 MoFo in-house counsel alumnae attended the MoFo Summit for Women held at the Ritz-Carlton in Half Moon Bay. This year’s theme was Focus on the Future – Empowering Female Leaders.

  • 2016 Bay Area Corporate Alumni Gathering

    On Thursday, October 27, 2016, MoFo’s Bay Area Corporate alumni met at Wine & Wall to reflect on their MoFo moments and achievements and catch up with former colleagues and friends.

  • 2016 Palo Alto Office 30th Anniversary Open House

    On Thursday, September 29, 2016, MoFo’s Palo Alto office alumni, clients and friends of the Firm met at the newly renovated office to reflect on their MoFo Palo Alto moments and achievements over the past 30 years. In addition to celebrating successes and catching up with friends, guests had the opportunity to meet San Francisco Giants and World Series champion Jeremy Affeldt.

  • 2016 Bay Area Women Litigation Alumni Reception

    On Wednesday, June 15, 2016, MoFo’s Litigation Department and its women alumnae (2000-2015) met for an evening of networking on the rooftop of Hotel Vitale, San Francisco.

  • 2016 New York Litigation Alumni Reception

    On Wednesday, June 22, 2016, MoFo’s New York Litigation Department and its alumni met for a food truck rodeo and evening of networking in the Morrison Foerster New York office.
