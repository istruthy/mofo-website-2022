The finance industry is changing rapidly. Clients across the sector require sophisticated legal services that not only respond to these industry developments, but also proactively anticipate where the market is going. MoFo’s fully integrated team of lawyers collaborate globally and seamlessly across jurisdictions to help our clients address the factors that will drive their businesses forward. With our broad technical know-how and deep legal experience, we partner with clients to tackle their most challenging multijurisdictional issues, keeping them ahead of the market while also managing their risk.

Our team represents clients on the full range of financing, securities, and regulatory matters, helping them address and tackle critical risks in regions such as Africa, Asia, Europe, Latin America, the Middle East, and the United States. We advise many of the largest and most significant players in the finance and investments industries across:

  • Financial institutions
  • Private credit and direct lenders
  • Private equity sponsors and portfolio companies
  • Borrowers and issuers
  • Broker-dealers
  • Insurance companies
  • Trading markets
  • Investment managers
  • Fintech companies
  • Payment Processing companies

We provide financial services companies with sector-specific, pragmatic legal advice covering the full range of their business needs and issues:

  • Financial regulatory
  • Lending and financial transactions
  • Structured finance
  • Private equity finance
  • Derivatives
  • Capital markets
  • Mergers and acquisitions
  • Credit risk management
  • Insolvency
  • Investment funds
  • Project finance
  • Enforcement
  • Compliance and investigations
  • Dispute management and resolution

As clients grapple with the uncertainties of today’s market, our globally recognized lawyers also act as thought leaders providing insights into and analysis of the key legal issues and trends impacting the industry to help them make advantageous strategic choices.
