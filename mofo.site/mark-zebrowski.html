Senior Counsel | San Diego

Mr. Zebrowski is an experienced trial lawyer whose practice focuses on commercial litigation, real estate, section 17200 litigation, partnership and shareholder disputes, and legal malpractice defense. He has more than 35 years of experience representing clients across a variety of industries including professional services, financial services, real estate, construction, aviation, medical device, life science, manufacturing, government contracting, energy, and technology (hardware and software). 

Mr. Zebrowski, in addition to handling general commercial litigation matters, has handled a variety of disputes including breach of contract, unfair business practices, trade secrets, mergers and acquisitions, franchise, distributor, investor and shareholder, construction defect, professional liability, insurance, land use and environmental, and product liability matters. In 2016-2020, San Diego Best Lawyers recommended him for Bet-the-Company, and Commercial Litigation as well as Environmental, M&A, and Real Estate Law. In 2021, Mark was named “Lawyer of the Year” for Bet-the-Company Litigation by San Diego Best Lawyers.

Mr. Zebrowski has consistently been recommended by Legal 500 US, Best Lawyers in America, San Diego Daily Transcript as a top attorney in corporate and real estate litigation and Super Lawyers. He was named San Diego Real Estate Litigation “Lawyer of the Year” in 2015 and Mergers & Acquisitions Litigation “Lawyer of the Year” in 2017 by Best Lawyers.

Mr. Zebrowski is a member of the American Board of Trial Advocates (“ABOTA”); a founding Master in the Wallace Chapter of the American Inns of Court; a director of the Thomas Jefferson School of Law; a past president of the San Diego Chapter of the Association of Business Trial Lawyers; the former managing partner of the San Diego office; and has served on numerous other non-profit boards. He is admitted to practice in the State of California; in the Southern, Central and Northern Districts of California; in the Ninth Circuit; and before the United States Supreme Court.

Mr. Zebrowski has presented at numerous Continuing Legal Education seminars, including for the Lawyers Club, the Association of Business Trial Lawyers, the Consumer Attorneys of California, the American Inns of Court, Morrison & Foerster client seminars, and in-house training for clients and Morrison & Foerster attorneys.
