  • A sum in lieu of interest will be payable on amounts held in our general client bank accounts on the following basis:
    • Interest will be calculated on an interim basis on matters that have intermittent movements, or when payments are made from client funds or at the end of a matter, whichever is sooner.     • In normal circumstances, if the total amount of interest calculated over the course of a transaction is less than £20, no interest will be paid.
