李若木律师是美富（国际）律师事务所的合伙人。李律师的业务重点为并购、私募股权交易和风险投资。她在代表美国、中国与国际财务和战略投资人处理各类科技和生命科学领域的交易方面拥有丰富经验，该等交易包括上市及私人公司的并购、股份与资产收购、拆分、少数股权投资, 以及合资公司。

在加入美富前，李律师曾就职于一家国际律师事务所的硅谷办事处。她精通英语和普通话。

Ruomu is a partner whose practice focuses on complex mergers and acquisitions, private equity transactions, and venture capital investments. She has extensive experience representing U.S., China and international financial and strategic investors in connection with a broad range of transactions in the technology and life sciences industries, including public and private mergers, stock and asset acquisitions, divestitures, spinoffs, minority investments, and joint ventures.

Prior to joining MoFo, Ruomu worked in the Silicon Valley office of an international law firm. She is fluent in English and Mandarin.
