Our Paralegal Program

As a MoFo paralegal, you’ll gain hands-on experience working directly with partners and associates on a variety of matters. We insist on the highest professional and ethical standards, and use cutting-edge technology to handle client matters efficiently and accurately.

Regarding our most accomplished professionals, the firm’s Senior Paralegal Program recognizes distinctive commitment to the legal profession, including areas of specialized expertise within a particular practice area, and a dedication to client service.

Expect More

High Value on Top Talent

Our continued success depends on our ability to attract, train, and retain the best talent. Most of our paralegals hold undergraduate degrees from four-year institutions and/or paralegal certificates from an ABA-approved program. Some of our paralegals have advanced degrees and other certifications.

We look for candidates with outstanding academic records, work experience, and excellent written and verbal communication skills. Paralegals must possess good judgment, and be detail–oriented, well–organized, and able to manage multiple tasks. We’re committed to hiring the best candidates from diverse backgrounds.

Dedicated to Your Development

We offer education reimbursement and internal paralegal training to help you maintain your skills. You’ll also be encouraged to attend regular meetings and training sessions hosted by our practice groups.

The breadth of our practice permits our paralegals to gain invaluable, varied experience. Many of our paralegals go on to law school, business school, or other graduate programs. Past members of our paralegal team have found that our collaborative and innovative environment offers excellent preparation for other careers. Others choose to stay with us as career paralegals.

Ready To Apply?

We encourage a variety of non-legal business professionals to apply. Please provide a cover letter and resume with your application. Specific instructions can be found within each job posting. Applicants who are a potential fit are invited back to meet and interview with managers, supervisors, and peers with whom they will be working if they join us. We encourage you to take advantage of the interview process in order to learn as much as possible about us.
